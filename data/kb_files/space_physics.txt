Light is an electromagnetic wave.
The electromagnetic spectrum includes radio waves, visible light, and gamma rays.
A black hole has gravity so strong that light cannot escape.
The Big Bang theory describes the expansion of the universe from a hot dense state.
A light-year is a unit of distance.
Stars produce energy through nuclear fusion.
The Sun is a main-sequence star.
Planets orbit stars.
Comets are icy bodies that orbit the Sun.
Asteroids are rocky bodies mostly found in the asteroid belt.
