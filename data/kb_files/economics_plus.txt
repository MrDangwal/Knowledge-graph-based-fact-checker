A market economy allocates resources through supply and demand.
A command economy allocates resources through centralized planning.
Inflation reduces the purchasing power of money over time.
Deflation is a sustained decrease in the general price level.
Interest rates affect borrowing and saving decisions.
A tariff is a tax on imported goods.
A subsidy is financial assistance provided by a government.
A budget deficit occurs when spending exceeds revenue.
The stock market allows trading of company shares.
A recession involves a broad decline in economic activity.
