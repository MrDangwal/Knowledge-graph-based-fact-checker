Christopher Columbus led voyages across the Atlantic in the late 15th century.
Ferdinand Magellan's expedition achieved the first circumnavigation of the Earth, completed after his death.
James Cook mapped many areas of the Pacific Ocean during the 18th century.
Marco Polo traveled along parts of the Silk Road and documented his journeys.
Roald Amundsen was the first person to reach the South Pole.
Ernest Shackleton led expeditions to Antarctica in the early 20th century.
Zheng He commanded large naval expeditions for the Ming dynasty.
Lewis and Clark led an expedition to explore the Louisiana Purchase territory.
Ibn Battuta traveled extensively across Africa, Asia, and Europe.
Amerigo Vespucci explored South America and wrote accounts of his voyages.
