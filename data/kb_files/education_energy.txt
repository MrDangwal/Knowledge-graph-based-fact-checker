Primary education provides foundational literacy and numeracy skills.
Secondary education builds on core subjects and may include specialized coursework.
Higher education includes universities and colleges that grant academic degrees.
Renewable energy sources include solar, wind, hydro, and geothermal power.
Fossil fuels such as coal, oil, and natural gas are non-renewable energy sources.
Solar panels convert sunlight into electricity using photovoltaic cells.
Wind turbines generate electricity by converting kinetic wind energy into mechanical power.
Hydroelectric power uses flowing water to generate electricity.
Energy efficiency measures aim to reduce energy use while maintaining the same output.
Battery storage helps balance electricity supply and demand on power grids.
