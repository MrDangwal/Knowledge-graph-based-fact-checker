Bridges connect landmasses and allow transportation across obstacles like rivers.
Highways are major roads designed for long-distance travel.
Railways transport passengers and freight using trains on tracks.
Ports facilitate the movement of goods and passengers by sea.
Airports serve as hubs for air travel and cargo transport.
Water treatment plants remove contaminants to provide safe drinking water.
Power grids distribute electricity from generators to consumers.
Telecommunications networks transmit voice and data over long distances.
Public transit systems include buses, subways, and commuter rail services.
Urban planning guides land use and development in cities.
