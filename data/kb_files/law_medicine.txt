Criminal law defines offenses against the state and prescribes punishments.
A patent grants exclusive rights to an invention for a limited time.
A trademark protects symbols or words used to identify goods or services.
Vaccines help prevent infectious diseases by training the immune system.
Antibiotics target bacteria rather than viruses.
The liver plays a key role in metabolism and detoxification.
